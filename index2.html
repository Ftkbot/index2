Ukraine in maps: Tracking the war with Russia

國際新聞（英文原文）

The geopolitical landscape of Eastern Europe has been irrevocably altered by the protracted conflict between Ukraine and Russia. As the war enters its third year, the dynamics on the ground continue to evolve, with both sides employing increasingly sophisticated strategies and weaponry.

In a bold maneuver, Ukrainian forces recently executed a surprise attack across the border, penetrating up to 30 kilometers into the Russian region of Kursk. This incursion not only caught Russian defenses off guard but also necessitated the evacuation of nearly 200,000 civilians from the border areas. The Kremlin vehemently condemned this offensive, labeling it a "major provocation" that would have far-reaching consequences.

The ramifications of this audacious move quickly became apparent. Within a fortnight, Ukraine's top brass asserted control over more than 1,200 square kilometers of Russian territory, encompassing 93 villages. This territorial gain prompted both Kursk and Belgorod regions to declare a state of emergency, underscoring the gravity of the situation.

Military analysts posit that this counter-offensive serves a dual purpose. Primarily, it's an attempt to compel Russia to redeploy units from the east, thereby relieving pressure on the beleaguered Ukrainian defenses in that sector. Additionally, it's viewed as a strategic gambit to enhance Ukraine's leverage in any future peace negotiations.

The conflict took another dramatic turn in early May 2024 when Russian forces crossed the international border north of Kharkiv, Ukraine's second-largest city. This incursion resulted in the seizure of several villages and triggered a mass exodus of civilians. While Russia's primary offensive has long centered on the eastern Donetsk region, this latest assault represents one of the most significant ground operations since the war's inception, further stretching Ukraine's already tenuous front-line defenses.

As the conflict persists, the international community watches with bated breath, cognizant that the outcome of this war could reshape the global order for decades to come. The fog of war may obscure many details, but one thing remains clear: the repercussions of this conflict will reverberate far beyond the immediate battlefield.

單字解析

advancing

中文釋義：前進；推進

The troops were advancing rapidly towards the enemy lines.

翻譯：部隊正迅速向敵方陣地推進。

evacuated

中文釋義：撤離；疏散

The coastal areas were evacuated due to the approaching hurricane.

翻譯：由於颶風即將來襲，沿海地區的居民被疏散。

condemned

中文釋義：譴責；強烈批評

The international community condemned the use of chemical weapons.

翻譯：國際社會譴責使用化學武器的行為。

redeploy

中文釋義：重新部署；調動

The general decided to redeploy his forces to strengthen the defensive line.

翻譯：將軍決定重新部署他的軍隊以加強防線。

beleaguered

中文釋義：困苦的；飽受困擾的

The beleaguered city had been under siege for months.

翻譯：這座飽受困擾的城市已經被圍困數月。

片語解析

surprise attack

中文釋義：突襲；出其不意的攻擊

The enemy was caught off guard by the surprise attack.

翻譯：敵人被這次突襲打得措手不及。

declared a state of emergency

中文釋義：宣布緊急狀態

The governor declared a state of emergency due to the severe flooding.

翻譯：州長因嚴重洪水宣布了緊急狀態。

relieve pressure

中文釋義：緩解壓力

The new strategy aimed to relieve pressure on the frontline troops.

翻譯：這個新策略旨在緩解前線部隊的壓力。

crossed the international border

中文釋義：越過國際邊境

The refugees crossed the international border seeking safety in the neighboring country.

翻譯：難民越過國際邊境，尋求在鄰國的安全。
